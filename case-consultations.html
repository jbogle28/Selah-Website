Case Consultations

We provide expert clinical consultation for professionals and organizations navigating complex psychological cases. This service offers an objective, evidence-based second opinion to enhance clinical decision-making and professional oversight.

Areas of Consultation:

  • Complex Diagnostic Review
  • Trauma-Informed Care Pathways
  • Clinical Supervision Support
  • Workplace Mental Health Strategy
  • Forensic-Clinical Intersections
  • Treatment Resistance Review

Professional Collaboration

Consultations are designed for licensed mental health practitioners, medical doctors, and legal professionals requiring specialized psychological insight for their own casework.

Ethics & Privacy

All consultations are conducted within the ethical guidelines of the HPC (Cayman Islands). Identifying client information is de-identified unless formal release of information is provided.
