Group Psychotherapy in Grand Cayman

Evidence-based clinical support for anxiety, trauma, and personal growth.

Community Healing

The Power of Shared Experience

Group psychotherapy offers a unique environment where healing happens through connection. These facilitated sessions leverage collective wisdom and support, helping participants realize they are not alone in their journey.

Active & Upcoming Groups:

  • Anxiety & Resilience: Building practical tools for stress management.
  • Grief & Loss Processing: Navigating the journey of loss with others.
  • Social Skills & Identity: Enhancing interpersonal confidence.
  • Mindfulness Support: Group practices for grounded living.

Group Privacy & Ethics

Confidentiality is the foundation of group work. All participants sign a strict agreement to ensure the group remains a safe, protected space. Groups are kept small to ensure personalized attention from the clinical psychologist.

Why Group Therapy?

Groups offer a more affordable clinical pathway while providing a sense of belonging and multiple perspectives on shared challenges.
