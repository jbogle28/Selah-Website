Psychological Assessments

Comprehensive diagnostic evaluations and testing services in George Town.

Diagnostic Services

Clarity Through Evaluation

We provide comprehensive psychological evaluations designed to offer clarity on complex clinical presentations. These assessments are essential for accurate diagnosis, treatment planning, and educational or workplace accommodations.

Areas of Specialization:

  • Diagnostic Clarification: Resolving uncertainty in clinical symptoms.
  • Adult ADHD Screenings: Evaluating executive function and attention.
  • Clinical Personality Assessment: Understanding long-term behavioral patterns.
  • Trauma & PTSD Evaluations: Assessing the impact of past experiences.
  • Mood & Anxiety Testing: Deep-dive evaluations for depression and worry.

The Assessment Process

Evaluations typically involve a detailed clinical interview followed by standardized psychometric testing. Results are compiled into a formal clinical report with evidence-based recommendations for care.

Professional Referrals

We accept referrals from physicians, psychiatrists, and employers. If you require a report for a third party, please notify us during the initial intake.
