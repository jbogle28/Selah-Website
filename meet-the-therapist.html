My Background

I am a Clinical Psychologist with more than 25 years of experience in the mental health field. I have completed graduate training in Counselling and Clinical Psychology, earned a PhD in Clinical Psychology, and I hold postgraduate certification in Neuropsychology.

Throughout my career, I have provided comprehensive psychological services across a variety of clinical settings, including inpatient and outpatient mental health facilities. My work is grounded in evidence-based practice and a holistic understanding of human functioning, guided by a deep respect for each person’s values, strengths, and goals.

As a psychologist who is personally guided by her Christian faith, I work collaboratively with individuals to foster insight, resilience, and meaningful change. Faith and spirituality are integrated into treatment only when desired by the client, ensuring a respectful, ethical, and inclusive therapeutic environment for all.

I currently provide psychological services in George Town, Grand Cayman, working with individuals from diverse cultural and professional backgrounds throughout the Cayman Islands.
