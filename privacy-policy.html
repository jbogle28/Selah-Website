Privacy & Confidentiality Notice

Your trust is the foundation of the therapeutic relationship.

At Selah Therapeutic Services Limited, your privacy is a priority. We are committed to protecting the confidentiality of your personal information and maintaining the highest ethical standards in our psychological practice.

Legal & Ethical Standards

Our practice operates in strict compliance with international and local regulatory requirements for data protection and medical ethics:

Cayman Islands DPA

We adhere to the Data Protection Act, ensuring your personal data is processed fairly, securely, and for specified purposes only.

Clinical Standards

Regulated by the Council for Professions Allied with Medicine (CPAM) and the Health Practice Commission (HPC).

Our Commitment to You

What you share within the therapeutic space is confidential. Information is not shared with third parties without your explicit written consent, except in specific legal or safety circumstances (such as imminent risk of harm to self or others).

Secure Communications

For virtual sessions, we use secure, encrypted platforms that meet healthcare privacy standards. Records are stored in a protected, professional practice management system.

Your Rights as a Client

In accordance with psychological best practices and local law, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal records.
  • Be informed of how your information is used.
  • Request corrections to any inaccurate data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

If you have questions regarding the handling of your data, please ask during your consultation.
