Meet the Therapist

Hello, I’m glad you’re here.

I am the sole practitioner and founder of Selah Therapeutic Services Limited, where my mission is to provide safe, compassionate, and client-centered psychological support for adults seeking healing and personal growth.

My Background

I am a highly experienced practitioner with extensive training in clinical psychology.

I have experience supporting individuals across diverse backgrounds and life experiences. My training includes evidence-based therapeutic models such as CBT, mindfulness-based approaches, trauma-informed practices, and integrative methods that consider mind, body, and spirit.

My Approach

I believe therapy is a sacred space — a pause in the rush of life to reflect, breathe, and rediscover strength. My approach is:

  • Warm and non-judgmental
  • Collaborative and empowering
  • Culturally sensitive and inclusive
  • Holistic — honoring emotional, physical, and spiritual wellbeing
  • Trauma-informed and paced for safety

I work with clients as equal partners. You bring your story; I bring a safe space, compassion, and the tools to help you find clarity and healing.

Why Selah?

The word Selah means pause and reflect, and that is the heart of this practice: A place where you can pause, breathe, and gain strength for your next steps.

When You're Ready

I’m here to support you. Whether you’re navigating a challenge or seeking deeper understanding of yourself, I would be honored to walk beside you on your journey.
