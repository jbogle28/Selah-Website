Welcome Message

Welcome to Selah Therapeutic Services Limited, a private psychological practice dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based mental health care for individuals seeking clarity, healing, and personal growth.

Why Selah?

The word Selah traditionally means pause and reflect—an invitation to slow down before moving forward with greater clarity and intention.

Every person deserves a place to slow down and be heard. Whether you're navigating anxiety, trauma, grief, or life’s transitions, you don’t have to walk this path alone.

How I Can Support You

  • Individual Therapy: Personalized, compassionate psychological support.
  • Trauma-Informed Therapy: Healing at a safe, respectful pace (e.g., EMDR, Attachment).
  • CBT & Mindfulness: Practical tools for daily wellbeing.
  • Spiritual Integrative Counseling (Optional): Honoring mind, body, and spirit.
