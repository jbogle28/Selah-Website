Employee Assistance Programme (EAP)

A streamlined, confidential programme providing short-term psychological support delivered exclusively by a registered Clinical Psychologist.

About the EAP Service

The EAP provides employees with access to short-term psychological intervention for personal and work-related concerns impacting wellbeing or performance. We focus on early intervention and practical strategies within an ethical framework, ideal for small to medium organisations.

Scope of Support

  • Work-related stress, burnout, and fatigue
  • Anxiety, low mood, and emotional distress
  • Adjustment to workplace or personal change
  • Interpersonal and relationship difficulties
  • Grief, loss, and trauma recovery
  • Psychological factors affecting performance

Confidentiality

Employee privacy is strictly maintained. No personal or clinical information is shared with the employer. De-identified utilisation data is the only information shared, ensuring a safe space for your team.
