Common Questions

How is my confidentiality protected? +

Your privacy is a cornerstone of the therapeutic relationship. All sessions are strictly confidential in accordance with the Health Practice Commission (HPC) guidelines and the Data Protection Act of the Cayman Islands.

What is the cancellation policy? +

A 24-hour notice is required for all cancellations. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours' notice may be subject to a fee.

What should I do in an emergency? +

Selah is an appointment-based practice. If you are in crisis, contact 911 or the HSA 24-hour Mental Health Hotline at 1-800-534-6463.
