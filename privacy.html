Privacy & Confidentiality Notice

At Selah Therapeutic Services Limited, your privacy is a priority. We are committed to protecting the confidentiality of your personal information and maintaining the highest ethical standards in our psychological practice.

Legal Standards

Our practice operates in compliance with applicable privacy laws and professional ethical guidelines, including:

  • HIPAA: Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act standards for protecting sensitive patient health information.
  • CPAM: Council for Professions Allied with Medicine standards and local regulatory requirements.

Our Commitment to You

What you share within the therapeutic space is confidential. Information is not shared with third parties without your explicit written consent, except in specific legal or safety circumstances as required by law (such as risk of harm to self or others).

Secure Communications

Messages sent through our contact forms or via email are handled with strict confidentiality. For virtual sessions, we use secure, encrypted platforms to ensure your sessions remain private.

Your Rights

As a client, you have the right to:

  • Request access to your records.
  • Request an accounting of how your information has been used.
  • Revoke consent for information sharing at any time.

If you have any questions regarding how your data is handled, please feel free to ask during your consultation or contact us at info@selahtherapeuticservices.com.

Email: info@selahtherapeuticservices.com

Hours: By appointment only

Privacy & Confidentiality: Your personal information and communication are protected under applicable privacy laws, including HIPAA and CPAM standards.

Crisis Support: Selah Therapeutic Services Limited does not provide emergency crisis services. If you are in immediate danger, please contact your local emergency number.
