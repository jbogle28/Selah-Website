Christian Integrative Counseling (Optional)

For clients who identify spirituality as a central part of their journey, this service blends psychological insight with respectful spiritual exploration.

Selah Therapeutic Services Limited offers optional Christian-integrated psychological services under the following conditions:

  • The request is initiated by the client
  • Written informed consent is obtained
  • Integration is clinically appropriate
  • Psychological treatment remains primary
  • Integration is discontinued upon client request

Note: Christian integration is used solely as a supportive psychological resource and does not constitute religious instruction, evangelism, or ministry.

Clients are not required to participate in faith-based elements to receive services. This service is entirely client-led and respectful of all faith and non-faith backgrounds.
